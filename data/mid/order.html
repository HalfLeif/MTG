Draftsim's Pick Order List for Innistrad: Midnight Hunt (MID)


Below you'll find Draftsim's draft rankings and ratings for every card in Innistrad: Midnight Hunt. These ratings are used in our draft simulator to inform the bot pick order within the packs. The ratings are curated by limited expert Bryan Hohns.

As always, a list like this is only a jumping off point to help you with Pack 1, Pick 1 decisions -- or maybe your first few picks. However, you should be quickly adjusting your picks according to curve, synergy, and signals from the other drafters. Feel free to share and discuss these ratings - I love talking limited!

If you want to use ratings on-the-fly while playing MTG Arena, just download our app Arena Tutor and it will automatically give you an overlay that shows you the rating and which card to pick!


-Dan from Draftsim
